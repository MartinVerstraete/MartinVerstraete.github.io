Welcome to EU Meat Visualization

The main purpose of this project is to visualize the imports and exports of meat in the European Union from 2012 to 2016. Our data are presented by two visualization tools, which are the heat map and the Sankey diagram. For the heat map, it shows the amount of meat imported and exported in kg per capita in different years.
On the top left-hand corner, you can select the type of meat, year and switch between imports and exports. The dimmer the colour means the more they exported or imported.
When you click on the map, a Sankey diagram will pop out on the right-hand side. It shows the distribution of meat imports and exports by the selected country.
